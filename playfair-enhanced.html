Input a keyword or keyphrase below. You can use nearly any alpha-numeric characters, including most punctuation. Duplicate characters are ignored when generating the table. The value you input here will determine the overall strength of the cipher. A short or unvaried keyword or phrase will make it easier to crack the code.



After clicking a button below, the digraph shows what the code does to your message before running it through the cipher. The asterix is trimmed out after deciphering and underscores are returned to spaces.
